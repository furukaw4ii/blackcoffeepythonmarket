# blackcoffeepythonmarket

This is a simple Python exercise I've made that simulates a shopping experience in our imaginary online store (but soon to be real!).

You can:

- Add products to your cart
- View your cart
- Remove products
- Proceed to payment
- Receive discounts based on your total purchase amount

We also have a Spotify profile, so you can listen to our music while shopping!

If you like alternative hardcore metal, search for:

'BLACKCOFFEE UNTITLED'

on Spotify and enjoy the experience.

I hope you enjoy this project!
It took me half a day to build and a lot of sweat and tears — but it was fun!!
